Our Process

From Kraft To Customer

A streamlined, quality-controlled manufacturing pipeline that ensures every box meets exact specifications.

Step 01

Design & Structural Engineering

Our in-house design team creates custom box structures using CAD software, optimizing for product protection, material efficiency, and print aesthetics.

Step 02

Raw Material Selection

Premium virgin kraft paper and high-quality recycled content are selected based on the required burst strength, flute type, and surface finish.

Step 03

Corrugation & Board Manufacturing

State-of-the-art corrugators produce single/double/triple-wall boards with precise flute profiles (B, C, E, BC, EB) for optimal strength-to-weight ratio.

Step 04

Printing & Die-Cutting

High-precision flexo and digital printers apply multi-color branding, while automated die-cutters create exact shapes, vents, and handles.

Step 05

Folding, Gluing & Finishing

Automated folder-gluers assemble boxes at high speed. Optional coatings, laminations, and reinforcements are applied for enhanced durability.

Step 06

Quality Control & Dispatch

Every batch undergoes stringent QC — burst strength, dimension accuracy, print fidelity, and moisture content — before dispatch.
